Tools to follow users on other instances

  • Graze for Mastodon (Chrome): Graze is 1-click follow, favorite, boost, and bookmark for Mastodon, no matter what instance you call home.
  • Graze for Mastodon (Firefox): Graze is 1-click follow, favorite, boost, and bookmark for Mastodon, no matter what instance you call home.
  • Mastodon4-redirect: A Chrome and Firefox extensions that redirects users from Mastodon4 instances to their home instance.
  • Roam with the Mastodon browser Extension: Share interesting links and sites. Follow anyone on any instance. Boost and favorite posts with one click.
